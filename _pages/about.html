LA Tactical Supply LLC
— Your Local Gun Shop

LA Tactical Supply LLC is a federally licensed firearms dealer (FFL #709824909) proudly serving Opelousas, Louisiana and the broader St. Landry Parish area. We carry a hand-picked selection of handguns, rifles, shotguns, ammunition, optics, and tactical accessories to meet the needs of hunters, competitive shooters, and everyday gun owners alike.

As a local FFL dealer, we're also set up to receive online firearm purchases on your behalf — making transfers fast, easy, and hassle-free. Our team is knowledgeable, friendly, and committed to helping you find exactly what you need.

We also provide gunsmithing services for cleaning, repairs, and custom work. Whether it's a simple cleaning or a more involved repair, we treat every firearm with care.
